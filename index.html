Built for the

ARRIVED was founded by Benjamin Chinyere Izuchukwu Ogojiaku in Lagos, Nigeria, with one clear mission: to give Africa's eHailing drivers a united voice in the media.

Most drivers lack the platforms to speak for themselves. Governments make decisions without hearing them. Platforms set fares without consulting them. We change that. Every story we publish, every driver we quote, every platform we hold accountable, is in service of the people who keep Africa moving.
